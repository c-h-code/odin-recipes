Boiled Water

This recipe is for people who need boiled water but have not done it before.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Heat Source
  • Pot

Directions

  1. Place water in large enough pot to hold 3 cups without boiling over.
  2. Turn burner on high.
  3. Do not watch pot or it will take forever.
  4. Do not leave unatended or you will burn the house down.
  5. Bring to a boil.
  6. Remove pot from burner.
  7. Turn burner off.
  8. Use immediately or you will have to repeat recipe.
